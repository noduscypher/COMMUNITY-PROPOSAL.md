# ☥ Digital Presence on the Reticulum Network

### A proposal for local businesses, spaces and communities

**From:** `noduscypher` · rawmesh-node · Beira Baixa, Portugal

---

> 🌿 **Status:** pilot phase with selected local spaces.
> If you are interested in joining, just reach out — we can explore it calmly together.

---

## What is this

The Reticulum Network is a decentralised communication infrastructure —
no central servers, no algorithms, no social media dependency.
It can run over internet, LoRa radio and local networks simultaneously.

Anyone with a compatible app installed — **MeshChatX**, **Sideband**, **NomadNet** —
can access pages, receive messages and communicate directly,
without intermediaries, without tracking, without permission from anyone.

---

## What we offer

### 📄 1. A page for your space

A dedicated page for your business, space or project,
accessible to anyone on the Reticulum Network.

It can include anything relevant to you:

- presentation of your space, project or services
- products, menu, offerings or schedule
- events, gatherings and announcements
- contact information and location
- your philosophy and values

Plain text. No cookies. No tracking. No algorithms. Just what matters.

---

### 📡 2. An LXMF announcement bot

An automated bot that sends direct messages to all subscribers
whenever you have something to share:

- new events or gatherings
- weekly specials or new products
- schedule changes or important announcements
- anything you want your community to know

Subscribers install the app once and receive your messages directly.
No spam. No algorithms. No middlemen.

---

### 🖥️ 3. Hosting on the rawmesh node

Your page and bot are hosted on the **rawmesh node** in Beira Baixa, Portugal,
with high availability.

No costs. No contracts. No conditions.

This is a contribution to the community — because local projects and honest spaces
deserve to be present on a resilient, open network.

---

### ☀️ 4. Future vision — solar and off-grid

When you are ready, you can have your own dedicated Reticulum node
powered entirely by solar energy.

A low-consumption Raspberry Pi connected to your solar panels,
serving your page and running your bots directly from your space.

- Minimal consumption
- Near-zero running cost
- Fully autonomous and resilient — even without grid electricity, even without internet

Your own presence on a resilient network, powered by the sun.
